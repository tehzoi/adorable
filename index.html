About Us

We're a bunch of friendly and experienced players who have decided to form our own place to stay and raid with people who share the same attitude towards raiding and clearing current tier /content with a decent pace without having to sacrifice real life activities and a place for people who got a life outside this game.

The core of this guild is people whom played alongside each other in previous tier and expansions, with a deep and vast experience when it comes to endgame content raiding.

Our Goal

We aim to clear current content and upcoming content, and to regularly achieve Cutting Edge once our core is completely established.

Since we're only raiding 2 days a week we have to make sure the time spent raiding is used well. We require everyone to be knowledgable of their class, learn mechanics fast, and be open minded for trying out new shit to kill a boss.

Beside raiding we're also interested in high mythic+ keys, and there's always someone who's up for making alt runs.

Updates
